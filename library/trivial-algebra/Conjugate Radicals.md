## Definition

## [[Hyperbolic Conjugate Radical]]

$$
(\sqrt{x^2+1} + x)(\sqrt{x^2+1} - x)=1
$$


## [[Series Reduction Conjugate Radical]]

$$
\frac {1}{\sqrt{x+1}+\sqrt{x}} = \sqrt{x+1} - \sqrt{x}
$$

## [[Trig Disguise Conjugate Radical]]

$$
\sqrt{1+x^2} \pm \sqrt{1-x^2} = 2 \pm 2 \sqrt{1-x^2}
$$
