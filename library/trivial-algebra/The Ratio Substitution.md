## Definition
For a [[Homogeneous Fraction]], all [[Variable]]s can be unified as n-1 Ratio.

$$
\begin{align}
&&&\text{let } y = k_1x, z = k_2x \\
&&&\frac{x^2+yx}{x^2 + y^2+z^2} \\
& \to && \frac{x^2+k_1x^2}{x^2+k_1^2x^2+k_2^2x^2} \\
& \to && \frac{1+k_1} {1+k_1^2+k_2^2} \\
\end{align}
$$
