## Definition
Identity Transform focuses on exact rewrites that preserve equality and create cancelable structure.

## When to Use

- A sum/difference structure appears and you want cancelable factors.
- The denominator is complex and needs rationalization or monomial reduction.
- You need exact equivalence without approximation error.

3. Finally, unify the expression via exponential-log conversion.
