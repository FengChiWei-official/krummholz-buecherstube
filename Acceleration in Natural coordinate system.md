## Definition

In [[Natural coordinate system]]

$$
\begin {align}
& \mathbf{a}  && = \frac{d(v \mathbf{\hat T})}{dt} \\
&&& = \frac{dv}{dt} \mathbf{\hat T} + v \frac{d \mathbf{\hat T}}{ds} \frac{ds}{dt} \\
&&& = \frac{dv}{dt} \mathbf{\hat T} + v^2 \kappa \mathbf{ \hat N} \\
&&& = a_T \mathbf{\hat T} + a_N \mathbf{ \hat N}
\end {align}
$$

where $a_T$ is the [[Tangential Acceleration]], $a_N$ is the [[Normal Acceleration]].

$a_T$ is easy to get directly by [[Speed]]($v$). $a_T = \frac{dv}{dt}$

$a_N$ is rather tough to get, $a_N = \frac{\mid \vec v \times \vec a\mid}{v}$
>except the [[Circular Motion]], $a_N = v \omega$.
