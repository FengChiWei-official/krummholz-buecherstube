## Definition

[[Radial Unit Vector]]: $\hat e_r$
[[Transverse Unit Vector]]: $\hat e_{\theta}$

We got $\frac{\hat e_r}{dt} = \omega \hat e_{\theta}$ and $\frac{\hat e_{\theta}}{dt} = -\omega \hat e_{r}$.
