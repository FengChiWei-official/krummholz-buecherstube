## Definition

Typical types:
**Velocity-dependent acceleration**

$a = f(v)$
$\frac{dv}{dt} = p(v)q(t)$
$\frac{dv}{p(v)} = q(t)dt$


**Position-dependent acceleration**
$a = f(x)$
$\frac{dv}{dx} \frac{dx}{dt} = f(x)$
$vdv = f(x)dx$
