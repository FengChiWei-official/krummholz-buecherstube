## Definition

basis is $F = ma$,

so $a = \frac {F}{m}$

$v = v_0 + at$
$p = p_0 + v_0 t + \frac{1}{2} a t^2$

## Cases

**No t**
$v_t^2 - v_0^2 = 2as$

**No a**
$\frac{v_t - v_0}{2} t = s$
